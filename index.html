What Is OaaS

Optimization Without the Overhead

Optimization as a Service (OaaS) is Pivotol AI's managed energy optimization offering. We deploy controlled infrastructure inside your operating facility — capturing only the datapoints that matter, validating every action through digital twin modeling, and tracking measurable P&L impact. No disruptions. No guesswork.

On-Premise Deploy

All optimization runs on your infrastructure. No cloud dependency, no external data transfer. Your facility, your data, your control.

Digital Twin Validation

Every optimization action is validated against a digital twin of your systems before execution. Data before capital investment, proof before commitment.

Verified P&L Tracking

Real-time financial impact tracking tied to actual kW/kWh reduction — not projected savings, but measured, verified results on your balance sheet.
